Warnings

If your HS Prim is Group deeded and you can no longer operate it, simply take it back into your inventory or sell it back to yourself and place it on the ground again. This will re-add you to the Operators list.
For attacks to work on Group land you will need to Deed the HS Prim to the lands Group. Make sure you are in the Operator list first by click Names / Operators on the menu.
Only an Officer or equivalent role in the Group can Deed an object to a Group.
  The command to make you an operator is: /8addo Your Name

Group Land / Group Operated / Group Safe

> Your Land IS Group Deeded?

Setting a Group and Deeding is needed. This is because SL only allows scripts to attack people when the land and script have the same exact owner. You need to follow steps 3 and 4 below.

> Your Land is NOT Group Deeded?

Setting a Group without deeding is only needed for those wanting to use Group Operated or Group Safe options. (If your land is owned by another avatar they must also own the HS Prim.)

> The following steps should be followed to make your HS Prim operable or safe for a Group and/or functional on Group owned land.
  1. If you want all Group Members & Officers be always be safe from the HS Prim when in Guest Mode:
    • click it and choose from the Menu: Settings / Weapon / GroupSafe and make GroupSafe on.

  2. If you want Group members & Officers to always be able to Operate the HS Prim:

    • click it and choose from the Menu: Settings / Security / GroupOp and make GroupOp on

  3. Setting the HS Prim's Group (in SL Viewer 1 or 2):
    • Right click and Edit the HS Prim.
    • Click the General tab
    • Click the Tick or Set button near "Group:"
    • select the correct Group and click OK

  4. If you have Deeded land:

    • Now Click the 'Content' tab.
    • Right Click 'Settings' & 'Channels' notecards and click Properties and tick "Share with Group" or "Group: Share".
    • Type the command: /8addo your name  - to make sure you are an Operator in the HS Prim.
    • Click the General tab, tick "Share with Group" or "Group: Share", click (Deed).
      (If Deed is greyed out you will need to contact a Group Officer and get permissions to Deed or pass the Prim to them and have them place it and Deed it for you.)
    • Right Click 'Settings' & 'Channels' notecards again and check Properties / "Share with Group" or "Group: Share" is still ticked. If not tick again.

    • Note: You no longer own the Prim, the Group does. SL sees Groups as seperate entities from avatars. To get the HS Prim back sell it to yourself or pick it up. You will need to be an Officer or have permissions within the Group to do so. If you have problems talk to a Group Officer. I will not be able to help you with Group permissions problems since I am not in your group.

  5. Now you can run SET UP from the main menu or use SETTINGS to adjust the HS Prim settings if you haven't already.

Unlimited Names

Groups can be used to have unlimited Operators, or GroupSafe visitors when GroupSafe is on. See Groups
  • You simply need a Role within the HS Prims group with ALL the "Allowed Abilities" UNTICKED.
  • Then you can add people to the group and make sure they only have that empty Role only.
  • Once done you can safely use the Group Safe or Group Operated options without compromising your land.
  • Make sure only authorised people can add members to the group.
  • Make sure to double check everything you have done, and make sure that it makes sense to you.
  • Lastly check that the Everyone role in your group has all abilities unticked.

I won't be reimbursing you if you give someone authority to do things you did not intend!
